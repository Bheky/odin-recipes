Lasagna

Description

A lasagna is a dish made from a mixture of pasta, cheese, and tomato sauce, often served with meatballs or other filling.

It is a classic Italian dish and has been prepared since the 16th century.

Ingredients
  • Meat
  • Pasta
  • Cheese
  • Canned Tomato
  • Onions
  • Fresh herbs
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Spices and Seasoning
Steps
  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place a large baking dish on a baking sheet.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the pasta, cheese, and tomato sauce.
  4. Add meatballs and cook for 3-4 minutes, or until meat is cooked through.
  5. Add onions, fresh herbs, and sugar to the bowl, and mix well.
  6. Add eggs and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until eggs are set.
  7. Mix the spices and seasoning in the bowl.
