Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is a versatile pasta dish that can be enjoyed with a variety of toppings and ingredients.

It's a quick and easy meal that's perfect for busy weekdays or when you need a quick snack.

Ingredients
  • 400g spaghetti
  • 4 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
Steps
  1. Boil a large pot of salted water and cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add the sliced garlic and red pepper flakes to the skillet, cooking until the garlic is golden brown.
  4. Once the spaghetti is done, reserve 1 cup of pasta water and drain the rest.
  5. Add the drained spaghetti to the skillet and toss to coat in the oil and garlic.
  6. If needed, add reserved pasta water to reach desired consistency.
  7. Season with salt, garnish with parsley, and serve with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
