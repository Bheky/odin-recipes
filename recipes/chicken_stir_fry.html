Chicken Stir-fry

Description

Chicken stir-fry is a yummy dish

Made with love!

Ingredients
  • 500g chicken breast, sliced
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots)
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, minced
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp cornstarch (optional, for thickening)
  • Cooked rice, for serving
Steps
  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat.
  2. Add the chicken slices and stir-fry until cooked through, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Add the minced garlic and ginger, and stir-fry for another minute.
  4. Add the mixed vegetables and stir-fry until tender, about 3-4 minutes.
  5. Stir in the soy sauce and oyster sauce, mixing everything well.
  6. If using, dissolve cornstarch in a little water and add to the mix to thicken.
  7. Serve hot over cooked rice.
